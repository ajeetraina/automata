# Sample Story Prompts for KidsStory AI

## Indian Folklore
- "Panchatantra: The Clever Crow and the Cunning Fox at Rameshwaram beach"
- "A young Ganesha helps village children solve a mystery in the forest"
- "Vikram and Betaal: The prince who learns the value of honesty"

## Animal Adventures
- "A baby elephant named Ellie who is different but saves the whole herd"
- "A penguin who wants to learn to fly and discovers something better"
- "The tortoise who wins the race not by speed but by never giving up"

## Magic & Fantasy
- "A girl who finds a library where books come alive at midnight"
- "The dragon who is afraid of fire and finds a new way to be brave"

## Values
- "Two brothers who argue over a mango tree and learn sharing multiplies happiness"
- "The seed that refused to grow until it understood patience"

## Quick Start
```bash
python3 pipeline/generate.py \
  --prompt "Panchatantra: The Clever Crow who outsmarts the Fox" \
  --duration 60 --style "colorful cartoon, Pixar style"
```
